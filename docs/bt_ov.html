take a look at 2. notice that "-" ? In balanced ternary, "-" is the lowest digit. Why? well as you might realize, its negative.

-
 this is the negative digit. otherwise called a negative charge.
Notice in the number table, how negative numbers start with it?
0
 this is zero. otherwise called the ground state
+
 this is the positive digit, otherwise called the positive charge.

here is a tip: the most significant digit  shows whether a balanced ternary integer is positive or negative.  For example in the integer "-++", "-" is the most significant digit, therefore the integer is negative.

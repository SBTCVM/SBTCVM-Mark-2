SBTCVM Settings

To toggle an on/off setting, click the switch button

To toggle a arrow selector setting, click either the left or right
arrows to cycle through the available options.

To change the CPU speed preset, click on the desired option.

To reset settings to standard defaults, click "reset". (you must
save for this to take effect!)

To save settings, click "save"
To quit, click "quit"
To cancel changes, click "quit", WITHOUT clicking "save".

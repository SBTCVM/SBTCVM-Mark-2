codeview help

scroll text: up/down keys or mouse wheel.
move text left/right: left/right keys
change font size: -/+ keys
Escape: quit
space: return to top-left of text file.
F8: save screenshot in "CAP" directory.

syntax highlighting:
text blocks are in blue.
comments are in red.
lines with |> refrences are hightlighted in green
lines refrence labels are hightlighted in blue
